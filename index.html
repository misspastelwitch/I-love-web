Hey there! I'm Stella, a 23 year old (aspiring) frontend designer based in the Netherlands. I've always been interested in design and aesthetics, and routinely find myself wishing websites were designed better. I hope that I can one day make people's experiences on the internet a little smoother, easier and prettier, because frankly there is not enough colour in the world.

Outside of making things look pretty, my greatest passion is travelling. I love discovering new cultures & tasting all the delicious foods this world has to offer. My favourite places I have been to are Hong Kong, not only for its super cheap street food and famous neon lights, but also for its amazing hikes, islands and of course, beaches.

what i'm working on

At the moment I am working on how to style websites in a cohesive manner. This includes making stylesheets, coming up with a house style, and mixing and matching different font faces & colours to evoke the right feelings for the page I'm creating.
